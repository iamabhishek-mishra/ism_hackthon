About MiningThon

India's Premier Mining Innovation Hackathon

MiningThon 2026 is a 48-hour national-level hackathon dedicated to solving real-world challenges in the coal mining industry — bringing together engineers, technologists, and innovators to reimagine mining for the 21st century.

Teams prototype solutions spanning mine safety, automation, environmental sustainability, and AI-driven resource optimization, with direct mentorship from Coal India experts and Ministry of Mines officials.
