2026 Tracks

Hackathon Themes

Four challenge tracks, each addressing a critical frontier in coal mining innovation. Pick your domain and build solutions that matter.

Mine Safety & Monitoring

Design real-time monitoring systems, wearable safety devices, gas detection networks, emergency alert platforms, or predictive hazard models that protect miners from accidents, cave-ins, toxic gas exposure, and other underground dangers.

IoT SensorsWearablesEmergency SystemsPredictive AnalyticsGas Detection

Automation in Mining

Build autonomous vehicles, robotic drilling systems, remote-operated machinery, conveyor optimization tools, or smart scheduling platforms that reduce human exposure to hazardous environments and increase operational throughput.

RoboticsAutonomous VehiclesRemote OperationsSmart SchedulingConveyor Optimization

Environmental Sustainability

Develop solutions for dust suppression, acid mine drainage treatment, land reclamation, water recycling, carbon footprint reduction, or renewable energy integration in mining operations to minimize ecological damage.

Dust ControlWater ManagementLand ReclamationCarbon ReductionRenewable Energy

AI in Resource Optimization

Apply machine learning, computer vision, digital twins, or data analytics to optimize coal extraction rates, predict equipment failures, improve geological surveys, and maximize resource utilization while minimizing waste.

Machine LearningDigital TwinsPredictive MaintenanceComputer VisionGeological AI
